Transport

Barcelona’s top tourist attractions are easily accessible by public transport having many bus and metro routes. Surprisingly Barcelona public transport system is not necessarily the best way to get around. Why? Because the city centre is not as big as you may think.

The metro is excellent and you won't have to wait more than 5 minutes for a train. However if you choose to walk you'll be surprised to discover that many sights in the city are a lot closer than you think.
