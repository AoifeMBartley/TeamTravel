La Sagrada Familia temple is an unfinished church of La Sagrada Familia which was designed by Antoni Gaudi in 1882. This unique and breathtaking construction is Barcelona's most famous tourist attraction and the most visited attraction in Spain. It is also rated as one of the world's top attractions by Tripadvisor users and welcomes 3 million visitors a year.
